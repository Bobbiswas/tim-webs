About Homeless Hope Uganda

Help the Society,Donate to the needy

HOMELESS HOPE UGANDA is a development focused initiative with its activities inclined to support the orphans and vulnerable children/child rights enhancement, advocacy, support and care to the young mothers, and evangelization of spiritual faith to the needy communities, capacity building, but also other income generating activities for sustainability.
Kyobe, 27 years old, is presently in charge of an orphanage, where he has 35 children under his care that are between 3 and 16 years old. He is currently in the process of building a better, bigger, more adapted building that will allow him to accommodate more Kids as their current lease is ending in January 2023 and they are feeling the pressure to relocate the children so they don't end up back on the streets.

The Project

There are three phases to this campaign to help get them back on their feet and self-sufficient

  • Phase 1
  • Phase2
  • Phase3

Approximately US$5,500

Acquire one acre of neighboring land to build the orphanage.

Construction of the New Building. Approximately US$12,000.

Acquire Beds, Mattresses, Sheets, Blankets, Pillows and Dining Tables. As well as anything to make the children feel comfortable and welcomed.

Grow food crops around the building on the one-acre property. With an abundance of fresh fruits and vegetables they will never have to worry again about hunger

Steps in order to start the Farm: Gather all the necessary farming instruments: rakes / shovels /garden hoses etc. Prepare and fertilize the soil and Purchase seeds and cuttings of: Fruits: Banana, Passion fruit, Watermelon, Pineapple Vegetables: Corn, Green Beans, Cabbage, Potato, Eggplant, Spinach as well as Sorghum and Cassava which we are less familiar with.
